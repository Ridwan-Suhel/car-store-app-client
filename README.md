# Car Store App

Welcome to my Car Store Application ‚Äì a robust, scalable, and feature-rich web platform designed to provide a seamless car purchasing experience. Users can browse and purchase cars effortlessly, update their profiles, and complete transactions with secure payment integration.

For administrators, the platform offers powerful management tools, including order tracking, user management, and product inventory control. Admins can upload and update car listings, manage customer orders, and oversee the entire sales process efficiently.

With a user-friendly interface and a streamlined workflow, our application ensures a smooth experience for both customers and administrators.

## Features 

### For Customers:
    - Car Browsing & Purchase ‚Äì Explore a wide range of cars and place orders seamlessly.
    - Secure Payment Integration ‚Äì Complete transactions safely with multiple payment options.
    - Profile Management ‚Äì Update personal details, and more
### For Admins:
    - Order Management ‚Äì View, process, and manage customer orders efficiently.
    - User Management ‚Äì Add, update, or remove users and control access levels.
    - Product Management ‚Äì Upload, edit, and delete car listings with detailed specifications.
### Additional Features:
    - Scalable & Secure Architecture ‚Äì Designed to handle growth while ensuring data security.
    - Responsive Design ‚Äì Works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
